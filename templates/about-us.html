Values

While developing Travel Bug, we had three values in mind towards the end product: -To learn - Travel Bug is our first project. Our original goal in this project has always been to learn key skills and about the app development industry. So you can use our product knowing that we have worked from a passionate mindset rather than a business one in delivering you our product. -For everyone - We wanted to develop a product that any modern individual can have access to use. Travel Bug is designed to be user friendly and fast to learn. We strived to develop an enjoyable experience. -To connect - Making an impact onto individuals is an impressive goal. However at (company name), we strive to aim higher. Travel Bug is aimed at not only individuals but groups of travellers. We want you to travel with your friends and family with a connected mindset where everyone can step into a completely new country or journey together.

Project Genesis

Travel Bug is our first project and is a platform designed for collaborative planning or trips or special occasiona. It seeks to eliminate problems in group communication. Group trip planning tend to result in long messy group chats or simply one individual planning everything for the trip. Travel Bug aims to rid of these issues and create a clear and simple way to plan as a group.

The future?

We plan to bring more features and functionalities to the Travel Bug project in future. Stay tuned!
