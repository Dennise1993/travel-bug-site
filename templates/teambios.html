Joshua Rodrigues
Founder - Manager
Joshua has always wanted to travel the world. He relishes in the joy of exploring new cultures and tasting new cuisines. He also loves playing around with new gadgets and testing out technology that will bring excitement and convenience into his life. Bringing his two loves together, Travel Bug was born.
