Making collaborative planning easy

Going on a trip with a group of your friends is always great fun and exciting. However the planning can involve messy groups chats, long back and forth conversions and avoidable miscommunications.

So we have built a platform to solve all of your travel planning problems.

Travel Bug helps you plan collaboratively with your friends and family or even your solo trips in an easy and effective manner. Keep all your important travel details organised.

We help you plan your adventures. So you can focus on exploring the world.

All your plans collated into a simple and easy to access format. We have iterated through countless designs to produce a UI that not only makes your life easier but also works.

Just punch in your plans and everything will be laid out seamlessly. Options are available to detail how you are planning to travel as well notes so you don't forget to deliver that important package!

Our designs ensure our app is both satisfying and easy to use.

View your plans from anywhere!

Jumping into a new country without an internet connection can be daunting! But don't worry. Travel Bug can be accessed anywhere without a travel connection. Just download your plans before jumping onto your plane and you're good to go!
