Making collaborative planning easy

Going on a trip with a group of your friends is always great fun and exciting. However the planning can involve messy groups chats, long back and forth conversions and avoidable miscommunications...

So we built a platform to solve all of your trip planning problems.

Travel Bug helps you plan collaboratively with your friends and family or even your solo trips and keeps all your important travel details in one organised platform.

We help you plan your adventures. So you can focus on exploring the world.

All your plans are collated into a simple and organised format. We have iterated through countless designs to produce a UI that not only makes your life easier, but also works.

Just punch in all the details and we will lay out everything seamlessly. Options are available to detail how you are planning to travel as well as notes so you don't forget to visit those hidden pubs you keep hearing about!

Our designs ensures our app is both satisfying and easy to use.

Our platform has been designed to focus on group travels. The process is simple:

  • Create a trip and share it with your travel companions.
  • Start planning together with our group focused functionalities.
