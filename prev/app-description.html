Making collaborative planning easy

Going on a trip with a group of your friends is always great fun and exciting. However the planning can involve messy groups chats, long back and forth conversions and avoidable miscommunications...

So together, we built a platform that solves all of your trip planning problems.

Travel Bug helps you plan collaboratively with your friends and family or even your solo trips and keeps all your important travel details in one organised platform.

We help you plan your adventures. So you can focus on exploring the world.

Our platform has been designed to focus on group travels. The process is simple:

  • Create a trip and share it with your travel companions.
  • Start planning together with our group focused functionalities.

Decide between bungee jumping or skydiving with a tap.

All your plans are collated into a simple and organised format. We have iterated through countless designs to produce a UI that will not only make your life easier, but also works. To get started:

  • Punch in the details such as location, time and activity
  • Add transport options
  • Add any nitty gritty details such as notes on how to enter that secret pub you keep hearing about!

Our designs ensures our app is both satisfying and easy to use.

View your plans from anywhere!

Jumping into a new country without an internet connection can be daunting! But don't worry. Travel Bug can be accessed anywhere without an internet connection. Just download your plans before jumping onto your plane and you're good to go!
